COAST GAURD Kindergarten

WE WELCOME YOU

A warm amiable and secure environment greets each one as they enter Coastguard kindergarten. Modern pedagogical practice state of the art infrastructure and an environment where the child will receive the best possible care , coupled with an atmosphere that fosters children's love of learning and encourages creative and fun. We also have a clean and child - centric facility, age specific classrooms and personal play area. You can see classrooms filled with happy, healthy children and enjoy loads of children art work throughout the facility. Our school keeps itself abreast with paradigm shift in the realm of education with their feet firmly rooted in values, ethics and traditional. The pupils stretch out their wings to soar the realm of the fast changing world, with their heads and hearts in the right place. The objective of Coastguard kindergarten is to Nurture individuals who are able to do extraordinary things in the most ordinary situations.

KNOW BETTER ABOUT US

Digital Classrooms

Well-equipped, IT-enabled classrooms with multimedia projectors to make the learning more interactive. Spacious and airy classrooms with custom designed furniture to suit student requirements.

Library & Activity Room

Well stocked library with carefully selected fictional and reference books across all ages. Students enjoy learning music, dance, dramatics and innovative art & craft activities!

History

Coastguard kindergarten was established in the year 1996 . It is located at Panipat Road, Hadoo , Portblair. The school started as a play school with a mere strength of 7 students. The school stands for academic excellence, development of skills and character formation with the view to groom the children and prepare them for primary school education

Academic Excellence

All our staff members are highly qualified, experienced and specially trained to enhance the children in every possible aspect. We have a group of well trained and qualified teachers who leave no stone unturned in the fulfillment of their duties. Their knowledge and teaching skills are upgraded periodically by conducting various seminars and workshops.

Curriculum

We prepare children to excel in kindergarten with a solid education foundation. Children from our school are thriving in primary school and beyond. We create opportunities for children to use their natural curiosity to lead them through imagination, questioning, learning and growing. At CGKG there is a perfect balance between play and learning.

Science Labs & Tech Center

Well equipped labs that aid hands-on learning to support the classroom learning. Integrates new technology into the learning experience. Modern technology center with latest computers to aid practical learning.
